This is about you & this is about others

The Fit Bundle is here for the fitness community and to benefit deserving charities

Mercy Ships

Mercy Ships manages hospital ships that provide free lifesaving surgeries for people where medical care is nearly non-existent.

Boma Project

BOMA helps ultra-poor women start small businesses in their rural communities, so they can pay for food, school fees and medical care for their families.

Exodus Road

The Exodus Road fuels interventions & rescues by providing covert gear, technology, resources & staff to support local authorities in finding and freeing modern slaves.

Camp Smile-A-Mile

Camp Smile-A-Mile provides challenging, unforgettable recreational & educational experiences for young cancer patients and more.
